LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Once you create an account, an approval request will be sent to admin. If that is approved, your account will be created.

You can have a maximum of 3 books ordered that have not been returned. You can keep a book with you for a maximum of 10 days.

If a book is not returned after 10 days, you will receive an email.

After the 10th day, a fine of 50RS will be imposed every day and on every book that has not been returned. This fine will be sent to you in the email every day.

If your fine exceeds 500RS or the book is damaged, the admin can block your account.
